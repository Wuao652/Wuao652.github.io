Research

My research leverages computer vision and audio processing to build real-world machine learning systems for applications in AR/VR, biodiversity and conservation problems. I'm also interested in exploring the potential of large language models(LLMs) to further enhance these capabilities.

Can Large Language Models Reason About Goal-Oriented Tasks?
Filippos Bellos, Yayuan Li, Wuao Liu, Jason J. Corso
ACL Workshop, 2024
project page / video / pdf
